## scope

Chapter 6 treats **database replication** for datasets fitting on one machine (lines 9168–11678). Replication purposes: geo-latency, availability, read scaling. Three algorithm families:

1. **Single-leader** — All writes to leader; followers replicate log; sync vs async/semi-sync; follower setup; leader failover; split-brain; fencing.

2. **Replication lag problems** — Eventual consistency; read-your-writes; monotonic reads; consistent prefix; causal consistency preview.

3. **Multi-leader** — Multi-datacenter; conflict resolution; last-write-wins risks.

4. **Leaderless** — Dynamo-style; quorums; read repair; anti-entropy; sloppy quorums; performance comparison.

Assumes full-dataset-per-node until Ch. 7 sharding. Backups distinguished from replication. [verified, lines 9168–11678]

## key_findings

1. **Replication goals** — Geo proximity, HA, read throughput; difficulty is handling changes. [verified, 9191–9215]

2. **Three families** — Single-leader, multi-leader, leaderless; all widely deployed. [verified, 9216–9219]

3. **Backups ≠ replication** — Replication propagates live changes; backups are point-in-time restore for deletes/errors. [verified, 9236–9247]

4. **Single-leader model** — Leader writes; followers tail log; reads any replica (may be stale). [verified, 9259–9307]

5. **Sync vs async** — Sync waits follower ack (blocks on failure); semi-sync one sync follower; async fast but data loss risk on leader death. [verified, 9315–9387]

6. **Quorum preview** — Majority sync replicas in consensus systems; forward ref Ch. 10 **[unavailable]**. [verified, 9369–9374]

7. **New follower setup** — Snapshot + log tail; consistency challenges. [verified, 9389+]

8. **Replication lag** — Followers behind; user-visible anomalies. [verified, 9757+]

9. **Consistency guarantees** — Read-your-writes, monotonic reads, consistent prefix, linearizability forward refs. [verified, 9757–10056]

10. **Multi-leader** — Per-region leaders; write conflicts; LWW dangerous; custom merge. [verified, 10057+]

11. **Leaderless/Dynamo** — W/R quorum overlap; version vectors; read repair; hinted handoff. [verified, 10686+]

12. **Quorums** — n replicas, w write acks, r read nodes; w+r>n for overlap; sloppy quorum trade-offs. [verified, 10795+]

13. **Performance** — Leaderless can be faster writes; coordination costs vary. [verified, 10962+]

14. **Eventual consistency confusion** — Author urges precision about guarantees. [verified, 9228–9234]
